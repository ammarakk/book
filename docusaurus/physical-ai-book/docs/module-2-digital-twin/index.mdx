# Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

Welcome to the second module of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics book. This module covers the concept of digital twins and how simulation environments like Gazebo and Unity are used in robotics development.

## Overview

In this module, we'll explore:
- Simulation environments for robotics
- Physics engines and 3D modeling
- Virtual testing and validation

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will understand:
- How digital twins accelerate robotics development
- The role of simulation in robot design and testing
- How to use Gazebo and Unity for robotics applications
